This Privacy Policy explains what data Nox Tarot ("Service") collects, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Data

  • Email address and account ID (Supabase Auth)
  • Display name and optional profile fields
  • Basic profile information from social login providers

1.2 Reading and Chat Content

  • Questions, concerns, and selected cards/spreads
  • AI-generated interpretation text
  • Reading usage history

1.3 Purchase and Subscription Data

  • Product ID, entitlement status, and transaction metadata
  • Payment processing details are handled by Google Play / RevenueCat
  • We do not store full credit card numbers
  • Subscription status, renewal dates, payment history

1.4 Device, Security, and Usage Data

  • IP address, user agent, app/browser version, language, timestamps
  • Device/browser signals and anti-abuse fingerprint values
  • Cookies/local storage (for session, preferences, and feature limits)
  • Page views and feature usage statistics (Google Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve readings, fortune features, and account services
  • Process purchases, verify entitlement, and manage usage limits
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Respond to support requests and service notices
  • Analyze service usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI Processing

To generate AI readings, prompts and related input content are sent to our AI provider (OpenAI).

  • Data sent to AI: question content, selected cards, spread type, language setting
  • Personally identifiable information (email, name, etc.) is NOT sent to AI
  • Do not include sensitive personal information in prompts
  • OpenAI does not use API input data for model training

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only when necessary:

  • With service providers that operate core app functions
  • To protect rights, safety, and service security
  • To comply with legal process or regulatory requirements
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer

6. Data Retention

  • Account and service data are retained while your account is active.
  • When you request account deletion, core account data is deleted as soon as technically possible.
  • Limited records may be retained where required for fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.
  • Reading history is stored in local storage on the user's device and is removed when browser data is cleared.
  • Server logs are retained for 90 days and then automatically deleted.

7. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication systems (Supabase Auth)
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Server-side management of API keys and sensitive information
  • Regular security reviews

The Service is not intended for children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover that personal information from a user under 18 has been collected, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
