Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

Nox Tarot provides digital content delivered immediately after purchase. This policy explains cancellation and refund handling for Google Play purchases.

Important

  • Google Play is the payment processor for in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Refund approvals are determined by Google Play policy and applicable law.
  • Canceling a subscription stops future renewals and keeps access until period end.

1. How to Request a Refund

For purchases made in the Android app, request refunds through Google Play:

  • Google Play Store > Profile > Payments and subscriptions > Budget and history
  • Choose the transaction and select refund/support options
  • Or visit Google Play Help Center

2. Product Types

Nox Plus (Monthly / Yearly)

  • Auto-renewing subscription billed by Google Play
  • Cancel anytime: Google Play Store > Subscriptions
  • Access continues until current billing period ends

Per-Reading Purchases

  • One-time purchase with immediate feature unlock
  • No auto-renewal
  • Refund follows Google Play policy and local law

3. Technical Issues

If charged but premium access does not activate, contact us with: Google Play order ID, purchase date/time, and a description of the issue.

noxtarot@gmail.com

4. Cancellation

  • Google Play Store > Subscriptions > Nox Tarot > Cancel
  • Cancellation stops future renewals only
  • Current paid period remains active until expiration

5. Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy limits rights that cannot be waived under your local consumer protection laws.
