Delete Account

Permanently remove your account and data

Warning: This action is irreversible

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your profile and account information
  • All tarot reading history
  • Payment records
  • AI usage data
  • All preferences and settings

Active subscriptions must be cancelled separately in Google Play Store.

Limited records may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or billing disputes.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Nox Tarot app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Type DELETE to confirm
  5. Your account will be permanently removed

Cannot access the app? Email noxtarot@gmail.com to request deletion.

Important Reminders

  • Cancel Google Play subscription first: Play Store > Subscriptions > Nox Tarot > Cancel
  • Account deletion does not cancel subscriptions automatically
  • Data cannot be recovered after deletion
